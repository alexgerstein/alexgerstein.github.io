About

Hi! My name is Alex Gerstein. I’m a software engineer, based in San Francisco. I write code in the hopes that others can spend less time at a computer. Currently, that means building better payroll at Gusto.

The content of this blog is entirely my own. I started posting here to formalize my learnings and further develop my writing.

When I’m not typing at a screen, I may be watching foreign films, playing a round of golf or reading fiction.
